Privacy Policy

Balance  ·  Last updated: June 16, 2026

Overview

Balance is a personal finance app for iOS. All data you enter is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal or financial information.

Data We Collect

We collect no data. Specifically:

  • Transaction records (titles, amounts, dates, descriptions) are stored only on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • No data is sent to any server.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.
  • No account or login is required.

Face ID / Biometric Authentication

If you enable app lock, Balance uses Face ID or Touch ID via Apple's LocalAuthentication framework. Biometric data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Balance does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers.

Data Deletion

All data is stored on your device. To delete it, uninstall the app. No action on our end is required or possible.

Children's Privacy

Balance does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top.
